---
title: Specialists vs generalists
publishedAt: 2026-05-15
kind: take
---

The framing is wrong. The actual axis is **range** vs **depth of one stack** — and nobody cares about your range until you've shown depth somewhere first.

Pick one thing you can be the person for. Then expand. Going wide before you've gone deep produces a CV that reads as "did six months of everything and stayed nowhere."

The exception: small teams where range is the product. But those teams hire based on what you've already built end-to-end, not on a stated preference for breadth.
