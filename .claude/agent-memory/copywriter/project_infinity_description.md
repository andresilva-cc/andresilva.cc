Infinity is one of the named projects on the /projects grid (and the home Now section). Its card description must describe it accurately.

**Ground truth (André-confirmed):** Infinity dispatches AI experts/agents into your Google Meet call. They listen to and follow the conversation, then contribute like a participant. Site: https://meet.agentairforce.com.

**Why this memory exists:** An earlier draft claimed Infinity "answers when called by name" — factually wrong. It does NOT respond by name, and it does NOT respond on detected silence/pauses either. The guidance: omit the trigger mechanism entirely — describe what Infinity *is*, not how it decides to speak.

**How to apply:** Never mention a trigger mechanism in Infinity copy (not "by name", not "on silence", not "when asked"). Describe the product: AI experts that join a Google Meet call, follow the conversation, contribute live. The shipped card description is `AI experts that join your Google Meet calls and contribute live` (63 chars, fits the [[project-project-card-descriptions]] two-line band) — reuse or stay consistent with it.
